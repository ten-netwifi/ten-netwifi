Quick Start

How to Connect

Get online in less than 2 minutes with our simple connection process.

1

Turn On Wi-Fi

Enable Wi-Fi on your phone or device and search for available networks.

2

Select Network

Choose "TEN-NET WIFI" from the list of available networks in your area.

3

Tap Sign In

Open your browser and tap the "Sign In" button on the landing page.

4

Choose Package

Select your preferred data package from our affordable options.

5

Enter Number

Input your M-Pesa registered phone number for payment processing.

6

Pay & Connect

Complete payment via M-Pesa and start browsing immediately!

Troubleshooting Guide

Can't Connect?

  1. Forget the WiFi network
  2. Turn WiFi off and on
  3. Reconnect to TEN-NET WIFI
  4. Open browser manually

Slow Speed?

  1. Check signal strength
  2. Move closer to the hotspot
  3. Clear browser cache
  4. Restart your device

Payment Issues?

  1. Check M-Pesa balance
  2. Verify phone number
  3. Wait for SMS confirmation
  4. Contact support if pending
